ELMS. FM. UID. ResLife. Bursar… UMD has its own slang, and some of it is quite confusing. There’s Testudo, the mascot who jumps around at sporting events; Testudo, the statue whose nose you should rub for good luck; and Testudo, the schedule of classes website. In this explainer, we review the most common acronyms and abbreviations for new students.

This short animation was created in Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro. It's exported as an MP4 file and closed captioned. It showcases motion graphics and mixed media I’ve been working on. I'm learning!
